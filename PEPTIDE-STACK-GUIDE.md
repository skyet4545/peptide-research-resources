# Research Peptide Stack Guide — Common Combinations & Protocols

This guide outlines commonly researched peptide combinations used in academic and self-research contexts. All information is for educational and research purposes.

## Popular Research Stacks

### 🔹 Healing & Recovery Stack
**Components:** BPC-157 + TB-500
**Rationale:** Complementary mechanisms — BPC-157 targets local tissue repair; TB-500 promotes systemic healing via actin upregulation
**Research Protocol:**
- BPC-157: 500 mcg/day (split into 2 doses)
- TB-500: 5mg 2x/week for 4 weeks (loading), then 2.5mg/week (maintenance)
- Duration: 4–8 weeks
- Full guide: https://peptideradar.net/blog/bpc-157-tb-500-stack.html

### 🔹 Growth Hormone Secretagogue Stack
**Components:** CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
**Rationale:** CJC-1295 extends GH pulse duration; Ipamorelin provides selective GH release without cortisol spike
**Research Protocol:**
- CJC-1295 (DAC): 2mg/week
- Ipamorelin: 200–300 mcg before sleep
- Duration: 3–6 months
- Full guide: https://peptideradar.net/blog/cjc-1295-ipamorelin.html

### 🔹 Cognitive Enhancement Stack
**Components:** Semax + Selank
**Rationale:** Semax provides BDNF upregulation; Selank modulates anxiety/GABA system
**Research Protocol:**
- Semax: 300–600 mcg intranasal, AM
- Selank: 250–500 mcg intranasal, PM
- Duration: 2–4 weeks on, 1–2 weeks off
- Guides: https://peptideradar.net/blog/semax-guide.html | https://peptideradar.net/blog/selank-guide.html

### 🔹 Anti-Aging Stack
**Components:** Epitalon + GHK-Cu
**Rationale:** Epitalon activates telomerase; GHK-Cu promotes cellular regeneration and collagen synthesis
**Research Protocol:**
- Epitalon: 10mg/day x 10 days (2x/year protocol common in literature)
- GHK-Cu: Topical or 1–2 mg subcutaneous 3x/week
- Guides: https://peptideradar.net/blog/epitalon-guide.html | https://peptideradar.net/articles/ghk-cu-peptide-guide.html

### 🔹 Fat Loss Stack
**Components:** AOD-9604 + Semaglutide (GLP-1)
**Rationale:** AOD-9604 targets fat metabolism specifically; GLP-1 agonists suppress appetite
**Research Protocol:**
- AOD-9604: 250–300 mcg/day subcutaneous
- Semaglutide: 0.25mg/week titrating up
- Guides: https://peptideradar.net/blog/aod-9604-guide.html | https://peptideradar.net/blog/semaglutide-complete-guide.html
